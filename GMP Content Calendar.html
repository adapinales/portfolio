07 — Rhythm Guide
Posting Rhythm Notes
How to Use This Calendar
  • Sunday is always a rest day — do not post
  • Monday opens every week with a Reel — highest reach day
  • Alternate Reel and Carousel through the week
  • Thursday and Friday are high-engagement days — use stronger hooks
  • Saturday content can be slightly more personal or story-driven
  • No more than two consecutive posts from the same pillar
Repurposing Notes
  • Every Reel script can become a Carousel on the same topic
  • Every Carousel can become a caption-only static post
  • Strong hooks from Reels become standalone caption openers
  • Week 1 Reels can be reposted as TikToks with no edits
  • Content bank ideas are pre-built for next month's planning
  • Save high-performing hooks — they become your testing baseline
