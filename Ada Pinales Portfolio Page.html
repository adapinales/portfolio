About

A Creator Who Solved
the Problem First

I didn't start as a content systems specialist. I started as a woman building a brand I believed in — The Girl Mompreneur — with no real system underneath it. Posting reactively. Creating without a framework. Spending more time figuring out what to say than actually saying it.

I solved that problem inside my own brand first. Unclear messaging, content overwhelm, inconsistent posting, no structure — I worked through all of it and built the pillars, frameworks, workflows, and AI processes that changed how I create.

That's the credibility. Not agency experience. Not years of client work. A creator who solved the problem first — and documented everything.

Now I build those same systems for women creators who are done reacting and ready to build from intention. I work remotely from Erie, PA using Claude, ChatGPT, and Canva. Every system is built to fit your brand specifically — not a generic template.
