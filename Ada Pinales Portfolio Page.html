About

A Creator Who Solved
the Problem First

I didn't start as a content systems specialist. I started as a creator with a brand I believed in and no real system underneath it — posting reactively, creating without a framework, and spending more time figuring out what to say than actually saying it.

Building The Girl Mompreneur required me to solve the same problems most creators face: inconsistent messaging, content overwhelm, unclear positioning, and creating without a system. Through that process, I developed content pillars, messaging frameworks, repurposing workflows, and AI-assisted content processes that changed how I create.

Now I build those systems for other creators — so they don't have to solve the same problems from scratch.

I work remotely from Erie, PA. I use Claude, ChatGPT, and Canva as my primary tools. Every system I build is documented, repeatable, and built to fit the specific brand — not a generic template.
