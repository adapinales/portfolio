02 — Stage One
The Insight & Hook
Stage 01 of 06
Capture the Insight — Build the Hook
Tool — Claude · ChatGPT
Every piece of content in this workflow begins with a raw insight — not a topic, not a title, but a real realization. Something observed, felt, or understood that the audience hasn't heard articulated clearly yet. Claude is used to sharpen that raw insight into a hook that creates instant recognition without giving everything away.
Claude Prompt Used
"I have this insight: [raw realization in my own words]. My audience is moms in an identity shift — they're figuring out who they're becoming and realizing the life they want requires a different version of them. My brand voice is warm, grounded, direct, and transformational — think Michelle Obama meets Erykah Badu. Write 5 hook variations for this insight. Each hook should land like a truth the reader has been feeling but hasn't said out loud. No questions. No hype. No generic motivation. Start with a statement."
Output from This Stage
  • 5 hook variations to test across formats
  • 1 selected hook that anchors the entire content suite
  • Insight sharpened into a single clear truth statement
03 — Stage Two
The Reel Script
Stage 02 of 06
Write the Reel Script
Tool — ChatGPT
Using the selected hook, Claude drafts a Reel voiceover script built around the GMP content formula — Realization, Explanation, Embodiment, Activation. The script is written to sound spoken, not read. Short sentences. Natural pauses. No filler. Under 60 seconds.
Claude Prompt Used
"Using this hook: [selected hook], write a 45–55 second Reel voiceover script in the GMP brand voice. Follow this structure: Realization (the hook + one sentence to land it), Explanation (2–3 sentences that break down why it's true using specific, real behavior — no abstract concepts), Embodiment (1–2 sentences showing what this looks like lived out — from personal experience or observation), Activation (one closing statement or question that creates a shift, not a to-do). Write it to sound spoken, not read. Short sentences. No filler words. No 'hey guys.' No call to action at the end."
Output from This Stage
  • Complete Reel voiceover script — ready to record
  • Formatted with natural pause breaks
  • Under 60 seconds when delivered at natural speaking pace
04 — Stage Three
The Caption
Stage 03 of 06
Write the Caption
Tool — ChatGPT
The caption is not a transcript of the Reel. It expands the idea in a different direction — adding a layer of personal context, a complementary truth, or a deeper explanation that rewards readers who stop to engage. Claude adapts the insight into caption format while maintaining the GMP voice.
Claude Prompt Used
"Using the same insight and hook, write an Instagram caption in the GMP brand voice. This is NOT a transcript of the Reel. It should expand the idea from a slightly different angle — adding personal context, a deeper truth, or a specific moment that makes it real. Format: first line is the hook (same or a variation), then 2–3 short paragraphs with a blank line between each, then one closing line that activates without being a hard sell. Conversational. White space. No hashtags in the body. Keep it under 200 words."
Output from This Stage
  • Full Instagram caption — formatted and ready to post
  • Distinct from the Reel script — adds value, not repetition
  • Under 200 words with intentional white space
05 — Stage Four
The Carousel
Stage 04 of 06
Build the Carousel
Tool — Claude · Canva
Claude structures the insight into a slide-by-slide carousel framework and formats the copy for direct use in Canva. Canva is used to design and format the slides using brand colors, typography, and layout — with Claude's output moving directly into the Canva template.
Claude Prompt Used
"Using this insight: [insight] and this hook: [hook], write a 6-slide Instagram carousel in the GMP brand voice. Slide 1: The hook — one line, bold, no explanation. Slide 2: Name the problem — what most women are doing or believing that keeps them stuck. Slides 3–4: Break it down — specific, real, no fluff. Each slide one clear idea, 1–2 sentences max. Slide 5: The shift — what changes when she sees this clearly. Slide 6: The activation — one question or statement she can sit with. No slide should have more than 2 sentences. Write for someone skimming at speed."
Output from This Stage
  • 6-slide carousel copy — structured and ready for design
  • Canva design produced using GMP brand colors and typography
  • Formatted for Instagram — optimized for mobile viewing
06 — Stage Five
Email & Community Content
Stage 05 of 06
Repurpose to Email or Community
Tool — ChatGPT
The same insight is adapted into a short-form email or community post — for a newsletter, a Skool community, or a free group. This version is more personal and conversational than the social content. It feels like a direct message, not a broadcast. ChatGPT handles the structural adaptation from social to long-form personal.
ChatGPT Prompt Used
"Adapt this insight into a short email or community post for The Girl Mompreneur audience — moms in an identity and income transition. Tone: personal, direct, warm. Write it like she's writing to one woman she knows, not a list. Open with the insight as a personal realization ('Something I've been sitting with...' or 'I keep coming back to this...'). 2–3 short paragraphs. Close with one question or invitation — not a sales pitch. Under 180 words total. No subject line needed."
Output from This Stage
  • Email or community post — under 180 words
  • Personal tone distinct from social content
  • Reusable for newsletter, Skool, or private community
07 — Stage Six
Future Offer Content
Stage 06 of 06
Seed Future Offer Content
Tool — Claude
Every strong insight is also a content seed for a future offer — a course module, a coaching prompt, a community challenge, or a product concept. Claude extracts the teachable core of the insight and maps it to a potential offer context. This keeps the content ecosystem connected to the business — not just the audience feed.
Claude Prompt Used
"Based on this insight: [insight], identify the teachable core — the specific transformation, belief shift, or skill this speaks to. Then suggest: (1) which GMP offer this could live inside as a module or lesson, (2) one discussion prompt this could become inside a community, (3) one challenge or exercise that would help a student embody this insight. Keep each suggestion to 2–3 sentences. This is for internal planning, not public content."
Output from This Stage
  • Offer mapping — which product this insight connects to
  • Community discussion prompt — ready to post
  • Student exercise or challenge concept
08 — Live Example
Workflow in Action
One insight run through all six stages — showing exactly what the workflow produces from start to finish.
Raw Insight
"Women keep starting over not because they lack discipline — but because their identity hasn't caught up to what they're trying to build."
Selected Hook
"Starting over isn't a discipline problem. It's an identity problem."
Reel Opening
"Starting over isn't a discipline problem. It's an identity problem. You don't keep quitting because you're weak. You keep quitting because you're asking a version of yourself that doesn't exist yet to do the work."
Caption Open
"Starting over isn't a discipline problem. It's an identity problem. I used to think I just needed more willpower. More structure. A better morning routine. What I actually needed was to stop trying to execute from a version of myself I hadn't become yet."
Carousel Slide 1
"Starting over isn't a discipline problem."
Email Opening
"Something I keep coming back to — most of us aren't starting over because we're undisciplined. We're starting over because we're trying to build from an identity we haven't stepped into yet. The plan isn't the problem. The becoming is."
09 — Workflow Summary
Time, Tools & Output
◎
Total Time
90–120 minutes per full workflow run — from raw insight to all six assets delivered and formatted. Carousel design in Canva is the longest step at 30–40 minutes.
◎
Tools Used
Claude or ChatGPT for hooks and insights. ChatGPT for Reel scripts, captions, and email adaptation. Claude for carousel copy and offer mapping. Canva for carousel design and visual formatting.
◎
Assets Produced
1 Reel script, 1 Instagram caption, 1 six-slide carousel, 1 email or community post, 1 offer content seed — from a single original insight.
For Clients — Replication Notes
"This workflow is brand-agnostic. It runs on any creator with a clear voice, defined pillars, and a real insight to start from."
What the Client Provides
Raw insight or content idea, brand voice reference doc, content pillars, and approval on the selected hook before the full suite is built.
What Gets Delivered
All six assets in a single Google Doc — organized by stage, formatted for use, and ready to post or hand off to a scheduler.
Turnaround Time
24–48 hours per full workflow run. Rush turnaround available at adjusted rate.
Customization
All prompts are adapted to the client's brand voice, audience, and platform before the workflow runs. No generic output.
